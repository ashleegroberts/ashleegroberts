Hey, I'm Ashlee.

Mom to a baby boy, farmer, sourdough baker, and content creator living a deliberately rooted life in Granbury, Texas. I share real resources for parents who want both intention and simplicity — in the baby years, in the kitchen, and on the farm.

🌿
My Story

The honest version of how I got here.

Honestly, farming was not my dream first — it was my husband's. Moving to Granbury and building Green Star Grove was an act of love, a choice to help him live out something he had always wanted. But somewhere between the early mornings, the chickens, and the sourdough that kept coming out better than the last batch, it became mine too. We started building Green Star Grove from the ground up: a small farm with pasture-raised chickens, a sourdough practice, and a farmers market booth that has become one of my favorite things about every other Saturday.

When our son was born, I threw myself into researching baby development the same way I research everything — obsessively, skeptically, and with a pretty low tolerance for vague advice. I read the actual science. I tried the activities. I figured out what was worth doing and what was just noise. Then I turned all of it into Play on Purpose: Baby's First Year, because I genuinely believe every parent deserves the information I had to dig for.

I also work as a UGC creator and brand ambassador for baby and lifestyle brands I genuinely believe in. Getting to make honest content about products I actually use with my son — no pretending required — is one of the better parts of this work.

Everything I share comes from real experience. That is the only version of this I know how to do.
