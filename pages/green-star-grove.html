From Our Flock

Pasture-Raised Eggs

Our hens roam on fresh pasture year-round — actual grass, actual sunshine, actual space to do what chickens do. The difference shows up in the yolk: deep orange, rich, and full of the nutrients that come from real outdoor living.

If you have only ever bought grocery store eggs, the first time you crack one of ours you will notice. That is not marketing — that is just what eggs look like when the hens live well.

From Our Kitchen

Artisan Sourdough

Long-fermented, naturally leavened sourdough made in small batches. Our starter has been fed and maintained for years, which means the flavor and texture you get in every loaf is the result of time and patience, not shortcuts.

We offer classic sourdough alongside our Spicy Monterey Jalapeño variety — which has developed a loyal following at the market, and for good reason.
