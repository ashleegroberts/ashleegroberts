About Ashlee

Mom, farmer,
relentless researcher.

I live on a small farm in Granbury, Texas with my husband and our son. We raise pasture-raised chickens, bake artisan sourdough, and sell at the local farmers market every other Saturday. I am also a content creator and brand ambassador for UGC Creator.

Everything I share — whether it is a baby development tip, a sourdough recipe, or a product recommendation — comes from genuine personal experience. I research obsessively, filter out the noise, and share what actually works in real life.
